Rebecca Reagan, Associate
 rebecca@garnerconsulting.com

Rebecca Reagan began her career in employee benefits in 2004. Thus far, she has experience in ratesetting, evaluating and quantifying the financial impact of alternative plan designs, and researching current regulatory developments. She has also assisted with claim auditing.

Ms. Reagan was previously employed in the non-profit sector, and has extensive experience with government regulatory language, including advising policymakers at the US Department of State. She has also evaluated insurer proposals for small benefit groups.

Ms. Reagan holds a B.A. from the University of California at Santa Cruz and an M.A from New York University. She has been a MacCracken Fellow, a Goethe Fellow and a Robert Bosch Foundation Fellow.
