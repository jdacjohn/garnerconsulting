Gerti Reagan Garner, President
 gerti@garnerconsulting.com

Ms. Reagan Garner, a CPA, CHFP (Certified Healthcare Financial Professional) and FLMI (Fellow of the Life Management Institute), has spent her professional career working in various aspects of health care management and financing. Her recent clients include Marriott, Southern California Schools Employee Benefits Association, Riverside County Employer/Employee Partnership for Benefits, Trader Joe's and Cap Management Systems.

Ms. Reagan Garner has conducted dental, disability, medical and managed care claim audits. She has also conducted health benefits strategic planning projects and organizational studies. In addition she has assisted clients during the implementation of new plans and administrators.

Ms. Reagan Garner began her career at Occidental Life Insurance Company where she was a Claims Examiner for five years. She progressed to Supervisor and, in that capacity, implemented the examining guidelines for the first automated gross utilization edits.

Prior to consulting, Ms. Reagan Garner served as Controller at Santa Teresita Hospital, where she was responsible for general accounting, patient accounting, admitting, outpatient and emergency registration, communications (PBX), data processing, utilization review, and DRG coordination. She also negotiated PPO/HMO and new physician contracts.

At Healthwest Associates, Ms. Reagan Garner was a Senior Financial Consultant responsible for managing client engagements, primarily assisting in pricing for contracts and reviewing contract proposals. Some of her internal projects included acquisition reviews, financial forecasts for Certificate of Need applications and feasibility studies. She developed forecasting models and methodologies that were adaptable to various applications.

As Controller at Maxicare, Ms. Reagan Garner supervised the conversion of the general ledger from one computer system to another. She also established procedures and documentation for commission payments and accounting. She integrated an acquired subsidiary’s accounting procedures and records with Maxicare’s main system. As a Senior Accountant at Coopers & Lybrand, Ms. Reagan Garner handled all phases of audit engagements, specializing in audits of health care institutions and insurance companies; she also audited employee benefit plans during her two and a half years with the firm.

Ms. Reagan Garner, a Certified Public Accountant in California, earned a B.S. degree (1978) in Business Administration, with an emphasis in accounting from California State University in Los Angeles. While there, she was on the Dean's List and became a member of Beta Alpha Psi, a national honor society in accounting, and Beta Gamma Sigma, a national honor society in business. She is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, Healthcare Financial Management Association, the Greater Los Angeles Claim Association and the Employee Benefit Planning Association of Southern California. She is past President of the Western Claim Conference and Southern California Chapter of HFMA (Healthcare Financial Management Association). She has authored articles published in Innovations in Human Resources, Newsbrief, Managing Employee Health Benefits and two chapters in the Health Insurance Answer Book. She has served as a guest lecturer on managed care at Chapman University.
